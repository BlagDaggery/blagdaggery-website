Dragon Slayer V2

Dragon Slayer V2 is my expanded version of the basic text game from the Codecademy lessons. I made it so that both you and the Dragon have 10 Health Points (HP), and the amount of damage done by both you and the dragon is random, giving you a greater chance of surviving the battle.
